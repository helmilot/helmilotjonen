2024 Aalto ARTS
Hanrock 25 This jewellery line was created as a side project for the Hanrock 2025 concert. The collection consists mainly of bracelets inspired by the event. Creating these pieces gave me the chance to improvise and to challenge myself as an artist. During this project, I focused on patterns and how changing them can create interesting results. Overall, mimicing, repetition, and staying focused were key outcomes of the process, well as understanding when it is the right moment to break the pattern. Despite the rain, the line sold out at the event and through social media! Big thanks to everyone who showed interest. This really motivated me to try new techniques and improve my self-expression.
