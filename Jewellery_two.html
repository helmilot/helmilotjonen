2025 Aalto ARTS
Hanrock 24 This was my first more formal jewellery experiment. The line consisted of earrings and was sold alongside the Hanrock concert in 2024. I discovered that making jewellery is a fun and meditative activity, and it also offers the opportunity to surprise yourself as an artist. Sold & celebrated It was amazing to see people get excited about my artistic output as wearable work. As always, dear friends remain the best supporters and give me the motivation to keep creating these projects. This project also taught me that experimentation is worth it. I learned to let go of strict planning and to trust the process. Mixing different materials added a lot of nuance and character to the pieces as well.
