As an MSc (Econ. & Bus. Admin.) and a Bachelor of Arts, I want to combine both sides. My skill set, bringing together analytical and creative abilities, offers an unusual combination. This website represents my artistic work but also serves as a coding experiment for further exploration. As a "from scratch" project, it has given me a chance to utilise and develop various skills. However, the project will not be finished; instead, the experimental pages act as a unique blog element that is constantly evolving.

I thrive at the intersection of strategy, analytics, creativity, and project ownership. I'm energized by complex, multi-stakeholder projects and enjoy turning abstract challenges into clear, actionable outcomes. My background spans leading student organizations, managing creative events, and delivering large-scale research while balancing multiple responsibilities. I'm driven by impact, continuous learning, and meaningful execution — and currently focus my work on internationalization projects.
