Hanrock is a concert created by four friends during the Covid pandemic. They concluded that people needed something to lift them beyond quarantine and boredom. Despite the restrictions, there was still the summer of Hanko —the sailing boats, and of course, Villa Gustava’s backyard. So what could one do with that?

The idea of Hanrock was and still is to perform familiar songs for friends. Our band has evolved from five members to seven, now also including a manager and a marketer (me).

See how the concert has changed over the years by clicking the upper button! The big numbers represent the amount of our audience each year. The other button shows photos from Hanrock’s six-year history. Some of the pictures were taken by Tobias Tarsaranta.

The first Hanrock took place in the yard of Villa Gustava, a property owned by friends. The idea was to play music for a selected group of close friends.

In 2021, the idea of a bigger concert was sparked. The concert would take place at Casino’s beach stage nearby. Every year since, it has continued to evolve and gain new features.

Coordinated marketing and managering starts. I step in as a graphic designer and organizer. First Hanrock-poster and Hanrock-Excel born.

First time add on Hanko’s website

The latest gig went under the name Hanrock Graduation. The theme was to celebrate all the past concert years. However, the concert faced the most difficult weather, which also affected the size of our crowd. Still, the atmosphere felt even more energized.
